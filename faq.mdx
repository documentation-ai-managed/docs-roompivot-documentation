## Photo Upload Requirements and Limits

Upload high-quality photos for best AI results. RoomPivot processes JPEG and PNG files under 10MB.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Supported Formats" icon="image">
    JPEG, PNG
  </Card>
  <Card title="Max File Size" icon="upload">
    10MB per photo
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recommended Resolution" icon="maximize">
    1920x1080 or higher
  </Card>
  <Card title="Aspect Ratio" icon="crop">
    16:9 or 4:3
  </Card>
</Columns>

Avoid blurry or heavily filtered images to ensure accurate staging.

## Common Issues and Resolutions

Encounter problems? Follow these steps to get back to redesigning rooms quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Fails" icon="upload-cloud">
    Check file size (`<10MB`) and format (JPEG/PNG). Resize images using tools like Photoshop or online compressors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Slow Processing" icon="loader">
    Clear browser cache and try incognito mode. Pro users get priority queue access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Poor Redesign Quality" icon="alert-triangle">
    Upload well-lit room photos without obstructions. Use the "Refine" button for adjustments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What if my subscription doesn't renew?">
    RoomPivot sends reminders 7 days before expiration. Renew manually in account settings or contact support@roompivot.com.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes, cancel from billing settings. Access remains until end of billing period.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Privacy and Data Handling

RoomPivot prioritizes your data security. Photos process on secure servers and delete after 30 days.

<Callout kind="tip">
  We never store or share your uploaded images without consent. Review our [Privacy Policy](https://roompivot.com/privacy) for details.
</Callout>

All transformations use anonymized data for AI training improvements. Enable "Private Mode" in settings to opt out.

For account deletion, email support with your user ID. Expect confirmation within 48 hours.
